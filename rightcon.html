Puzzle Modes

Easy mode:

  • Small grid (e.g., 4×4)
  • Numbers are in a more straightforward sequence
  • Minimal branching paths

Medium mode:

  • Medium grid (e.g., 5×5)
  • More twists and possible dead ends
  • Requires some planning ahead

Hard mode:

  • Large grid (6×6 or more)
  • Highly scattered numbers
  • Multiple “trap zones” to avoid
