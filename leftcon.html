Puzzle Rules and Regulations

Rules

  • You are given a grid of numbers.
  • Your goal is to connect all the numbers in the correct order to form a single continuous path.
  • The path must cover every cell exactly once.
  • Moves can only be made horizontally or vertically (no diagonals).
  • You can’t revisit a cell once it’s been used.
  • The puzzle is solved when all numbers are connected in the correct sequence.

Regulations

  • Start with unique numbers or numbers pairs that stand out.
  • Look for corner or edge constraints — they often limit moves.
  • Test possible paths but avoid ones that get “trapped” early.
  • Use the undo/reset option if you get stuck.
